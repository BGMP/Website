LOGOS & USAGE

A collection of our main assets, crafted by the talented Hylia, which you can download to integrate with RVA or link back to us!

Allowed Usage

  • Use our logo to link to our website
  • Use our logo to advertise that you integrate with RVA
  • Use our logo in blog posts or articles about RVA

Forbidden Usage

  • Use our logo as your application's icon
  • Create a modified version of any of our logos
  • Integrate any of our logos into your logo
  • Use any of our artwork without permission
  • Change the colours, dimensions or add your own text/images

Please contact us

  • If you want to use artwork not included in this repository
  • If you want to use these images in a video/mainstream media

Naming Projects and products

  • Please avoid naming your projects anything that implies {{ site.org }}'s endorsement. This also applies to domain names.

Attribution

  • Other assets like car rating images, track images, emojis, etc. are made by members of our community, and all rights belong to their respective authors
  • These guidelines were heavily based on GitHub's Logos and Usage Guidelines
